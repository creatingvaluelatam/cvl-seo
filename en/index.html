Creating Value Latam (CVL)

Creating Value Latam is a regional initiative focused on advancing value creation, co-creation and value capture in organizations across Brazil and Latin America.

CVL connects scholars, executives and institutions interested in strategy, innovation, services and digital transformation, fostering dialogue between academic research and managerial practice.
